MeJuvante Private Limited Privacy Policy

Scope. This policy applies to our internal application that automates posts to the official MeJuvante LinkedIn Company Page.

1) Who we are

MeJuvante Private Limited (“we”, “us”) operates internal tools to streamline publishing to our official LinkedIn Page.

2) What data we collect

We do not collect personal data from public users through this page or application. The tool is used by authorized staff only.

3) How we use LinkedIn

  • Create and publish posts on behalf of our Company Page.
  • Store post content, media, and scheduling metadata needed for publishing.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data.

4) Third-party services

Publishing uses LinkedIn’s APIs and infrastructure. Your use of LinkedIn is also governed by LinkedIn’s own policies.

5) Security

Access is limited to authorized employees. Credentials are stored securely and rotated when necessary.

Questions or requests: mj_collaboration@mejuvante.com
